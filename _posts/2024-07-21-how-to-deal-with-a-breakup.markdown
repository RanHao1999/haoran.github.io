I have just experienced a sad breakup and I think I am qualified to explain it.

Tip No.1: Go no contact.
This might be struggling at the beginning since cutting a part of your heart off so fiercely is definitely a torment.
However, te first one or two weeks are the most crucial.
Delete all the platforms and all the ways of contact to force yourself to get used to the life without her.

Tip No.2: Focus on other things.
